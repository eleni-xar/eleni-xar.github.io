Welcome to Fun1-land!
A world of resources on maths and programming

Fun-land is a magical island where you can escape wherever you feel like to.2 There live our friends: numbers, functions, strings, types and all the other maths and programming beings. They pass their day playing games, solving puzzles, and exploring the world around them. They are very curious, and they wish to know everything that can be known about their world. They are also very friendly and welcome anyone who shares the same curiosity as they do. Fun-land is full of little paths, twists, and corners which await you to investigate them and discover their secrets. The Fun-landers will be there to help you all along the way. On the way, you'll see for yourself how beautiful and fun programming and maths can be.

1: In case you wonder, the 'Fun' in Fun-land comes both from fun (obviously), but also from fun-ctional programming.

2: The photo is actually from Fiji, but trust me, Funland is equally charming.

We visit Fun-land very often; we are fascinated by its wonders and beuty. To help other visitors we teamed up with the Fun-landers and created some resources which will help you navigate their world.
